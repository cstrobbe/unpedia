  • de Maupassant, Guy: Boule de Suif. Correspondance. Étude de Pol Neveux. Œuvres complètes de Guy de Maupassant. Paris: Louis Conard, 1908.
    This volume contains a four-page biography of Guy de Maupassant, an eighty-page essay by Pol Neveux, a previously unpublished section of correspondance, the text of “Boule de Suif”, the article “Les Soirées de Médan” Maupassant wrote for the newspaper Le Gaulois (17 April 1880) and an appendix with several other previously unpublished stories: “La Main décorché”, “Le Donneur d'eau bénite”, «Coco, Coco, Coco frais!», “Le Marriage du lieutenant Laré”.
