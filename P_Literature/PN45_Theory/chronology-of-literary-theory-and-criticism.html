Chronology of Literary Theory and Criticism

1916
Lukács, Georg [György Lukács de Szeged]: Die Theorie des Romans. Ein geschichtsphilosophischer Versuch über die Formen der großen Epik (The Theory of the Novel) (first translated into English in 1971)
1920
Eliot, T. S.: The Sacred Wood, containing the essays “Hamlet and His Problems” and “Tradition and the Individual Talent”
1924
Richards, I. A.: Principles of Literary Criticism
1927
Forster, E. M.: Aspects of the Novel
1929
Richards, I. A.: Practical Criticism
Bakhtin, Mikhail: Problems of Dostoevsky's Poetics (first translated into English in 1973)
1930
Empson, William: Seven Types of Ambiguity (second, revised edition 1947, reprinted 1949)
1932
Leaves, F. R.: New Bearings in English Poetry
1936
Brooks, Cleanth: An Approach to Literature
1938
Brooks, Cleanth; Warren, Robert Penn: Understanding Poetry
Ransom, John Crowe: The World's Body
1941
Ransom, John Crowe: The New Criticism
Burke, Kenneth: The Philosophy of Literary Form
1943
Ransom, John Crowe: A College Primer of Writing
Brooks, Cleanth: Understanding Fiction
Wellek, René; Warren, Austin: Theory of Literature
1947
Brooks, Cleanth: The Well Wrought Urn
1950
Trilling, Lionel: The Liberal Imagination: Essays on Literature and Society. (A collection of sixteen essays that had previously been published in various journals.)
1952
Leavis, F. R.: The Common Pursuit
1957
Frye, Northrop: Anatomy of Criticism: Four Essays
1958
Williams, Raymond: Culture and Society
1961
Lewis, Clive Staples: An Experiment in Criticism
Girard, René: Mensonge romantique et Vérité romanesque
1966
Burke, Kenneth: Language as Symbolic Action: Essays on Life, Literature and Method
Derrida, Jacques: La structure, le signe et le jeu dans le discours des sciences humaines (“Structure, Sign, and Play in the Discourse of the Human Sciences”), included in L'écriture et la différence (1967).
1967
Derrida, Jacques: De la grammatologie (Of Grammatology)
Hirsch, Eric Donald: Validity in Interpretation
Jauß, Hans Robert: Literaturgeschichte als Provokation der Literaturwissenschaft
1969
Kristeva, Julia: Séméiôtiké: recherches pour une sémanalyse
1970
Barthes, Roland: S/Z
1981
Jameson, Frederic: The Political Unconscious: Narrative as a Socially Symbolic Act
1982
de Man, Paul: The Resistance to Theory, Yale French Studies, 63
1989
Ashcroft, Bill; Griffiths, Gareth; Tiffin, Helen: The Empire Writes Back: Theory and Practice in Post-Colonial Literature
Veeser, Harold: The New Historicism
