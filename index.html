About this Site

This website is a growing uncyclopedia of links. It is also an experiment in using Library of Congress Classification for organising information; hence the strange-looking URLs.

Certain topics are not covered on this site because they are covered on other websites:

  • Language Learning,
  • Philosophy and Science.
