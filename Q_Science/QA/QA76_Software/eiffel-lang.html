Eiffel Programming Language

Eiffel is an object-oriented programming language designed by Bertrand Meyer. One of its basic principles is design by contract, a concept that was also formulated by Bertrand Meyer. Meyer also wrote several books, including Object-Oriented Software Construction (first published in 1988) and Touch of Class: Learning to Program Well with Objects and Contracts (Springer, 2009).
