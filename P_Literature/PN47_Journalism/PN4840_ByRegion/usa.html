Journalism in the USA

Independent or Alternative Media

  • Democracy Now!: news program hosted by Amy Goodman and Juan Gonzalez. See also Democracy Now! on YouTube.
  • Thom Hartmann Program is a radio program with a blog and a YouTube channel with thousands of videos.
  • The Young Turks was founded by Cenk Uygur, who was left MSNBC in 2011 after he was demoted because he asked politicians too many critical questions. The Young Turks (TYT) has a network of channels on YouTube, including the following:
    • The Young Turks,
    • TYT Investigates,
    • TYT Interviews,
    • TYT Nation, hosted by Jeff Waldorf and
    • TYT Politics.
    See also: Eric Blattberg: The Young Turks is running circles around news networks on YouTube (DigiDay, UK, 31.10.2014).
  • The Jimmy Dore Show is an irreverent and humorous take on todays headlines and hypocrites. See also The Jimmy Dore Show on YouTube, where Jimmy Dore pulls no punches. Jimmy Dore is also a host on The Young Turks.
    (In April 2018, CNN deceptively called the Jimmy Dore Show a far-left YouTube channel that peddles conspiracy theories, such as the idea that Syrian chemical weapons attacks are hoaxes. This statement was heavily criticised by Cenk Uyghur on the Young Turks channel.)
  • The David Pakman Show started in 2005 as a community radio show and is now is a daily internationally syndicated politics and news talk show airing on radio, television, and the internet, and on Free Speech TV via DirecTV and DISH Network. See also The David Pakman Show on YouTube. (The David Pakman show reached 500,000 subscribers on 15 August 2018.)
  • The Ring of Fire started in 2004 as a weekly syndicated radio show and is now a multi-media network for the latest Progressive news, commentary and analysis. Hosts on the show include Farron Cousins, Thom Hartmann, Sam Seder, David Pakman and Mike Malloy. See also The Ring of Fire on YouTube, where news is mostly presented by Farron Cousins.
  • The Majority Report with Sam Seder is a daily political talk show hosted by Sam Seder. See The Majority Report with Sam Seder on YouTube.
  • The Humanist Report is a progressive political podcast that analyzes political news stories through the lens of humanism. The podcast is hosted by Mike Figueredo, who is pursuing a Ph.D. in public policy. See The Humanist Report on YouTube and The Humanist Report on SoundCloud. Mike Figueredo has also written a few articles for HuffPost.
  • Secular Talk is a radio show run by Kyle Kulinski. See also Secular Talk on YouTube.
  • The Real News is video-news service that reports with ordinary people’s interests in mind. See also The Real News on YouTube.
