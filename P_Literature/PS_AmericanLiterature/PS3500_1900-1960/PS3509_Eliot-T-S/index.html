    A collection of essays from the years 1917 to 1961 that includes “The Aims of Education”, “To Criticize the Critic”, “From Poe to Valéry”, “American Literature and the American Language”, “What Dante Means to Me”, “The Literature of Politics”, “The Classics and the Man of Letters”, “Ezra Pound: His Metric and Poetry” and “Reflections on Vers Libre”.
