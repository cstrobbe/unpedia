Chinese Literature

Classical Chinese Literature

  • Wikipedia: Chinese literature: Classical fiction and drama.
  • Wikipedia: Classic Chinese Novels.
  • Wikipedia: Romance of the Three Kingdoms (14th century, attributed to Luo Guanzhong).
  • Wikipedia: Water Margin (story set in the Song dynasty; written in the 14th century, attributed to Shi Nai'an).
  • Wikipedia: Journey to the West (story set in the Tang dynasty; written in the 16th century, attributed to Wu Cheng'en). See also A Supplement to the Journey to the West, (written around 1640) which does not have the same status as Journey to the West.
  • Wikipedia: Jin Ping Mei (written during the late Ming dynasty).
  • Wikipedia: Dream of the Red Chamber (also known as The Story of the Stone; written in the middle of the 18th century by Cao Xueqin).
  • Wikipedia: The Scholars (story set in the Ming dynasty; written in the 18th century by Wu Jingzi).
