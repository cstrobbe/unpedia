Online Resources

Below are other fitness and (physical) health channels that are worth watching:

  • Bob & Brad is a channel by Bob Schrupp and Brad Heineck, two physical therapists from the USA. They humorously call themselves the two most famous physical therapists on the Internet. They aim to offer the best "get fit , stay healthy, and pain-free" information directed toward people 0 to 101 years old. Their videos cover topics such as pain relief, stretching, abdominal and core strengthening, posture, sleeping positions and flat feet. The also have a website at BobAndBrad.com.

Training Advice

  • Le gainage, c'est bien? Parlons en! (Major Mouvement on YouTube, 3 minutes, 31.03.2018.
    This video demonstrates several alternatives to traditional (static) planks: gainage pas de l'ours, gainage latéral (raise one arm above your head while turning your body), gainage tape épaules, gainage pompes coudes, gainage stabilité chevilles, gainage push main push coudes, gainage dead bug, gainage combat and gainage fessiers.
  • Chris Hemsworth's Workout Explained By His Personal Trainer | Train Like a Celebrity (Men's Health on YouTube, 6 minutes, 27.11.2018).
    Luke Zocchi, Chris Hemsworth's personal trainer, explains the workout he designed for Hemsworth for the film Men in Black: Internationalk (2019). The workout was designed to achieve a less bulky look than Thor and to be doable while travelling, so it involves only bodyweight and a pair of dumbbells. It starts with a warm-up that involves bear crawls (10 rounds of 20 seconds, with resting periods of 10 seconds) and bodyweight squats (also 10 rounds of 20 seconds, with resting periods of 10 seconds). The other exercises are weighted burpee (8 reps), curl to press (8 reps), goblet squats (8 reps), standing triceps extensions (8 reps), reverse lunge curls (8 reps), a shoulder excercise that cycles through a lateral raise, a front raise and an upright row (8 reps), and three plank variations: plank punches (8 reps), plank pulses (8 reps) and plank pikes (8 reps). This set of exercises are done in a cycle for thirty minutes.
    Warning: Skip or replace the upright rows in the shoulder exercise; the upright row demonstrated in this video raises the elbows above shoulder level, which brings your shoulders into internal rotation, and this can be harmful to the shoulder joints.
