Literature Stack Exchange Reading Challenges

The monthly topic challenges are a result of the feedback on the question Do we want something akin to topic challenges for books outside our bailiwick, to help alleviate the book diversity problem? The outcome was overwhelmingly positive. Some other sites run weekly topic challenges, as described on Meta Stack Exchange: How do weekly topic challenges work? However, due to the time it takes to read a book (added to the time it takes to borrow it from a library or to buy it), weekly topic challenges would be too short. Suggest your Lit.SE reading challenges here! is a meta post where participants can submit new proposals for monthly challenges. Monthly Topic Challenges aren't getting much attention. How can we change them? is a meta post from July 2019 asking for proposals to increase contributions to the monthly challenges.

There were no reading challenges in December 2017 and January 2018. For each topic challenge, the number of questions, answers, views, participants and votes refer to the activity during the topic challenge. Answers, votes, views etcetera that were submitted later don't count, even though it would be interesting to see which topic remained “popular” after the topic challenge.
