A Companion to “Shakespeare and His World”

Introduction

This collection of web resources serves as a “companion” to the MOOC Shakespeare and His World, which had its fifth run on FutureLearn from 20 April till 27 May 2017. The course was created by Professor Jonathan Bate (Warwick University) in co-operation with the Shakespeare Birthplace Trust.
