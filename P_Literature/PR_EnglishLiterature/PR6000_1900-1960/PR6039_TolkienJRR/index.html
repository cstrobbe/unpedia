John R. R. Tolkien

General Links

  • Tolkien Estate: the official website of the J.R.R. Tolkien Estate.
  • Tolkien Society: an educational charity, literary society, and international fan club, devoted to promoting the life and works of J.R.R. Tolkien. (a registered charity in England and Wales).
  • Wikipedia: The Middle-earth Portal.
  • Guide to The Lord of the Rings, Wikibooks.
  • Tolkien Studies: An Annual Scholarly Review: a journal published by West Virginia University Press. The founding editors are Douglas A. Anderson (The Annotated Hobbit), Michael D. C. Drout (Beowulf and the Critics) and Verlyn Flieger (Splintered Light: Logos and Language in Tolkien’s World).
  • Journal of Tolkien Research (JTR), a peer-reviewed open-access journal.
  • TheOneRing.net: a site by and for fans of J. R. R. Tolkien's works.
  • The One Wiki to Rule Them All.
  • Tolkien Gateway: a wiki-based Tolkien Encyclopedia.
  • TolkienWiki.
  • Societatea Tolkien din România: the Romanian Tolkien Society.
  • Mythopoeic Society: The Mythopoeic Society is a non-profit organization devoted to the study of mythopoeic literature, particularly the works of members of the informal Oxford literary circle known as the “Inklings.”
  • The Flame Imperishable: A blog about Tolkien, St. Thomas, and other purveyors of the Philosophia Perennis by Jonathan S. McIntosh.
  • The Tolkien Bookshelf: website about out of print, used, and rare books by, or relating to, J.R.R. Tolkien.
  • Tolkien: The Official Bookshop (by the publisher HarperCollins).
  • Middle-earth Enterprises (formerly known as Tolkien Enterprise): a company that owns exclusive worldwide rights to motion picture, merchandising, stage and other rights in certain literary works of J.R.R. Tolkien.
