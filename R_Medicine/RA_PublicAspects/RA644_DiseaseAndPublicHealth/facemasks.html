  • DIY — make protective mask (Cuisine Test on YouTube, 10 minutes, 18.03.2020).
    This video is in French and shows how you can make a masks without a sewing machine. Among other things, it shows how you can achieve the same type of stitches that a sewing machine would make.
  • A Doctor Explains How to Make the Safest Face Mask ( Dr. Ryan Southworth on YouTube, 15 minutes, 22.03.2020).
    This video shows how you can create the type of HEPA filter that is sometimes used in vacuum cleaners. See also the directions on Google Drive, template for the masks (on Google Drive) and the CDC webpage “Strategies for Optimizing the Supply of N95 Respirators” cited in the video. (The latter page was updated after the video was put online.)
    Note that a number of other sources say that filters from vacuum cleaners are not appropriate for breathing through.
