Usability

General Resources

  • Laws of UX: Laws of UX is a collection of the maxims and principles that designers can consider when building user interfaces. The website created by Jon Yablonski. For a quick overview, see The Laws of UX - 19 Psychological Design Principles ( Joseph from LearnSketch.com on YouTube, 10 minutes, 11.06.2019).
  • Usability Body of Knowledge: a website created and maintained by the User Experience Professionals' Association (UXPA).
  • Usability.gov: the US government's usability website.
  • Interaction Design Pattern Library: website by Martijn van Welie, interaction design consultant at Philips Design.
  • UI Style Guides: website by the user experience consultancy Experience Dynamics (Portland, Oregon, USA).

Pie Menus

  • Hopkins, Don: The Design and Implementation of Pie Menus, Dr. Dobb's Journal, December 1991.
  • Hopkins, Don: Pie Menus: A 30 Year Retrospective, Don Hopkins on Medium, 15.05.2018.
  • Satanek, Brandon: Fitts’s Law, Pie Menus, Gestures and a Car Touchscreen Experience, EXPERIENCEDzine, 10.02.2015. See also the video Fitts’s Law, Pie Menus, Gestures and a Car Touchscreen Experience (YouTube).

Fitts's Law

  • Goktürk, Mehmet: Fitts's Law, The Glossary of Human Computer Interaction.
  • Tognazzini, Bruce: A Quiz Designed to Give You Fitts, Ask Tog, February 1999.
    This article is a must-read if you want to test your understanding of Fitts's law.
  • Atwood, Jeff: Fitts' Law and Infinite Width, Coding Horror, 09.08.2006.
  • Halsband, Ulrike: Fitts’sches Gesetz, Dorsch Lexikon der Psychologie, 2014, 2020.
  • Hale, Kevin: Visualizing Fitts's Law, Particletree, 03.10.2007.

Interaction Design

  • Thimbleby, Harold: Press On: Principles of Interaction Programming. MIT Press, 2007. (528 pages) ISBN 9780262514231.
  • Cooper, Alan: The Inmates Are Running the Asylum. Sams, 2004. (288 pages) ISBN 978-0-672-32614-1.
  • Dubberly, Hugh: Alan Cooper and the Goal Directed Design Process, Dubberly Design Office, 01.03.2001. (Originally published in Gain AIGA Journal of Design for the Network Economy, Volume 1, Number 2, 2001.)
  • Collins, Hugh: Six UX Books Not Written by Don Norman, Alan Cooper, or Steve Krug UX Pros Need to Read, UX Booth, 04.09.2019.
  • Understanding Personas – An Interview with Alan Cooper (InfoQ on YouTube, 66 minutes, 01.10.2014).
  • Alan Cooper talks UX, Design and Environment with ProjectUX (ProjectUX on YouTube, 13 minutes, 21.11.2017).
  • Universal Principles of Design: website for a set of books on design concepts, especially The Universal Principles of Design by William Lidwell, Kritina Holden and Jill Butler (Rockport Publishers, 2010).

Other Links

  • World Usability Day: annual event that always takes place on the second Thursday in November. Many local events have their own website, for example, World Usability Day Stuttgart.
  • User Experience Professionals Association (UXPA). See also the UXPA chapters and SIGs.
  • Berufsverband für User Experience und Usability Professionals | German UPA
  • Open Source Design: a community of designers and developers pushing more open design processes and improving the user experience and interface design of open source software.
  • Pande, Jagriti: Heuristic Evaluation : A Guide For Beginners and Experts, UX Gorilla, 17.03.2018.
  • Muniz, Fabio: An Introduction To Heuristic Evaluation, Usability Geek, 30.05.2016.
  • Nielsen, Jakob: Summary of Usability Inspection Methods, Nielsen Norman Group, 01.11.1994.
  • Nielsen, Jakob: 10 Usability Heuristics for User Interface Design, Nielsen Norman Group, 24.04.1994.
